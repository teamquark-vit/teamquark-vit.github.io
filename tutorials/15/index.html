Design Tips

UI/UX Design Tips / Volume 2

A designer’s most challenging task? Creating an innovative design concept. This talent can’t be learned, only obtained gradually through experience. With tips and tricks, we can help guide and hone your vision.

In this video, we’ll discuss interface pictograms. It’s our strong belief that pictograms fulfill an integral functional role. We’ll teach you how to highlight design accents intelligently and creatively, how to present your work to clients, or simply prepare projects for a portfolio. Finally, we’ll address visual hierarchies and how to present information in the ideal sequence. This concerns building mobile interfaces as well as web-based ones.
